NPA's Speedcore Haven

SubSpeedcore

SubSpeedcore is music that has a BPM of 299 or lower.

Speedcore

Speedcore is music that has a BPM of 300 or higher, though the default range is 300-599.

Splittercore

Splittercore is music that has a BPM of 600 or higher, though the default range is 600-999.

Extratone

Extratone is music that has a BPM of 1000 or higher, though the default range is 1000-14999.
